Wave-treeclose

Introduction

Wave-Tree allows the management and control of real-time production progress. It is a lean and effective tool for following the work in progress of phases and materials and receiving data on activities from machinery or from declarations of human resources on board the machine. The completely web and “touch ready” interface allows operators to declare in real time all the process information that cannot be automatically detected (setup, production, unloading of input materials and declaration of output materials, scraps, human resources, etc.).
